# Discord commands

Commands:
  ^join       Receive a session link
  ^players    List all players that have joined
  ^online     List all players that are currently online
  ^rooms      List all room IDs

# In-game commands

Commands:
  /spawn                        Warp to spawn
  /room <room_id>               Warp to room
  /rain                         Toggle rain globally
  /snow                         Toggle snow globally
  /skin <skin_id>               Change skin
  /home <player>                Warp to home of player
  /give <item_id> <quantity>    Give item to yourself
  /clear                        Clear your console
  /owner                        Show the room owner
  /host                         Show the room host
